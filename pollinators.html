Did you know 58% pollinators are declining in Ontario?

Bees, butterflies, and other pollinators are essential for biodiversity, but their numbers are declining rapidly.

Without them, many plants—including the food we eat—will struggle to survive.

🌼 How Can You Help?

✅ Plant native flowers – Provide nectar and pollen sources.

✅ Grow a variety of blooms – Different pollinators need different flowers.

✅ Avoid pesticides – Keep pollinators safe from harmful chemicals.

✅ Create a bee-friendly space – Leave wild areas for shelter and nesting.

🐝 Every garden makes a difference! Start planting today to help save pollinators!
